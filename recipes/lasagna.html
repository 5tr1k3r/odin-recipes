Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef and onion until cooked through.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and parsley.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Add a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of ricotta mixture.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Bake for 45 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
